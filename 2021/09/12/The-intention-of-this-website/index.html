The intention of this website

Created2021-09-12|Updated2021-10-01
|Post View:|Comments:

This is the first article of this website and I feels every happy about it. Honestly it cost me near a week to final deploy it onto the github and published out, but thankfully I finally finish it. I gained a lot in this building process, about computer science, about program communities and so on. Now you may ask, why you want to build this website which so difficult for me to do? Well, the answer is simple: I want a place which is easy to access and that I can share my life experience, opinions and emotions in it. At the end I want to give a big thanks for all the people who post the ways to build a website and ways to solve problems during the coding, thank you all for teaching me, a novice of coding and internet techniques. I hope to see you in the next blog post and thank you for spending your time reading this.
